# EDA — Rossmann Store Sales
## Key Findings
- Dataset: 1,017,209 rows, 1,115 stores, Jan 2013 - Jul 2015
- Usable rows after filtering closed stores: ~844,338
- Promo uplift: +38.8% average sales increase
- Day of week strongly affects sales (Sunday highest, Saturday lowest)
- Store Type B generates ~47% more revenue than other types
- Missing values in CompetitionDistance (3), CompetitionOpenSince (354), Promo2 fields (544)
